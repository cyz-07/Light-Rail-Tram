## A mod that adds much needed trams and light rail into the game

## Tram
**Features:** 
At-grade crossings, but way slower than light rail due to mixed traffic (16km/h)
Lower capacity compared to light rail (1 car only and 214 passangers), but also lower costs
Should only be built at-grade (non at-grade costs are x100, switch to light rail to build not at-grade)

## Light Rail
**Features:**
Completely seperated from traffic with its own right of way, thus faster (70 km/h)
Higher costs comapred to trams but higher capacity (225 passangers) and has the ability to have 2 cars

## Both
**Features:**
Both are able to use each others track interchangeably
